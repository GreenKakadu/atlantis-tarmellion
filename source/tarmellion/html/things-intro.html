Things of Tarmellion

You can get information on most things that exist in Tarmellion here. More or less complete lists of the following things are there:

  • ressources : the raw materials of Tarmellion; the things that can only be produced if a certain hex has this type of ressource;
  • products : the made goods of Tarmellion; everything that has to be made out of other material and that is no weapon, armour or structure; only the basic goods are listed here to preserve a few surprises
  • weapons : weaponry of Tarmellion; the things to hurt one another; only basic weapons are listed here to safe some discoveries for you
  • armour : armoury of Tarmellion; the things to prevent weapons from hurting the wearer; only basic armour is listed here to keep a few secrets till later on
  • buildings : architecture of Tarmellion; all the types of buildings that can be errected in the lands of tarmellion
  • ships : the mobile structures of Tarmellion; the things that can be sailed
