Products of Tarmellion

Here are listed all the lesser products of the game of Tarmellion. They can be produced out of the available normal ressources and sold to the city markets.

Tool specifies the item that helps produce this item. A unit with the needed skill will get a bonus of man months listed behind the tool if he uses that tool while producing the product.

Value is the amount of silver one of these items is worth. This is used when calculating the price of the product in city markets or the amount of the item gotten in monster spoils. It also is used when the positive effect of trade (buying AND selling) for the city growth is calculated each month.
