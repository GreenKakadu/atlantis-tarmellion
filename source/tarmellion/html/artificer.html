Artificer

The artificer is a jack of all tades. He is an able craftsman who can create all products available in Tarmellion. Be it tools, weapons or armour or even trade goods from stone, herbs, wood and so on. Also building a hut, a road or a castle is no difficulty for him.
Artificers excel in the production of goods, out of their midst come the greatest smiths, architects and every other Master Craftsman.
