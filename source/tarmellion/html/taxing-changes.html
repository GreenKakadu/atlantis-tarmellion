Taxing Changes

Taxing is a topic I had nearly overlooked here. It wasn´t really changed, but reorganized for Tarmellion. From the possibilities the standard game engine offers the following choices have been made:

  1. Combat Skill

    Everybody who knows how to fight may also tax.

  2. Archery and Crossbow Skills

    Also those that know how to fight with ranged weapons can tax.

  3. Riding Skill

    Knowing how to use a mount in combat is impressive enough to tax too.

  4. Magic Skills

    Being a mage will get a peasant to pay taxes.

  5. Ownership of weapons

    A peasant asked to pay taxes will not demand to see if the tax collector can really use his weapons.

  6. Ownership of magic battle items

    Having a staff known to bring death should be enough to cow a peasant into paying taxes.

  7. Ownership of monsters

    Riding a dragon or a war mammoth is another sure way to get the tax payer to cooperate.
