Skill Changes

There are some new skills in Tarmellion that are described here.

  1. Scouting

    The scouting skill is like stealth in regard to visibility by other units. But having a scouting skill level even to the observation skill level of a guarding faction will allow access.
    You cannot steal or assassinate with it though.
    The second ability of this skill is a saving on the monthly upkeep cost. Each skill level with save two silver.

  2. Animal Training

    This displaces the old horsetraining skill. It is used to "produce" animals that are available as resources in a hex and that cannot be slaughtered for food afterwards.

  3. Animal Taming

    Animal taming depends on the animal training skill. Meaning you need to study animal training before you can learn animal taming.
    It is used to further train animals gotten with animal training into animals that aid in combat like chargers or war elephants.
    Its second use is to produce combat animals like fire drakes or unicorns that are available as resources in hexes.
    This skill is only available for good factions.

  4. Monster Taming

    Monster taming depends on the animal training skill. Meaning you need to study animal training before you can learn monster taming.
    It is used to further train animals gotten with animal training into monsters that aid in combat like war horses or war mammoths.
    Its second use is to produce combat monsters like fire lizards or chimeras that are available as resources in hexes.
    This skill is only available for evil factions.

  5. Smithing

    This skill replaces the old armoursmith and weaponsmith skills. it is used to produce all normal weapons and armour.
    It is also used to produce metal tools.
    And it is the foundation for both armourcraft and weaponcraft.

  6. Armourcraft

    This skill depends on the smithing skill.
    It is used to produce the advanced types of armour like admantium chain mail.

  7. Weaponcraft

    This skill depends on the smithing skill.
    It is used to produce the advanced types of weapon like admantium daggers.

  8. Toolmaking

    This skill replaces the old carpentry skill. It is used to produce non-metal tools like spinning weels or barrels.
