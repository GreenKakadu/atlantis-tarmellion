Healing Changes

The way healing works has been modified in Tarmellion.

  1. Additional items with healing capacities

    Herbs, fungus, medicine plants, healing drafts, medicines and healing potions have healing abilities.

  2. Number of men healed

    How many injured men are healed is no longer dependant of the item used, but of the skill level of the healing skill.

    • Healing skill: for each skill level up to 5 men can be healed
    • Healing Potion: one healing potion can heal up to 10 men with a chance of success of 100% and does not need the healing skill to be used
    • Magical Healing Skill:
      at skill level 1, a mage can heal up to 10 men with a chance of success of 50%
      at skill level 3, a mage can heal up to 25 men with a chance of success of 75%
      at skill level 5, a mage can heal up to 100 men with a chance of success of 90%

  3. Chance of success

    The chance if healing is successful is dependant on the item used.

    • Magical healing does not used up any items and has differeing chances of success dependant on the skill level in magical healing:
      skill level 1 = 50%
      skill level 3 = 75%
      skill level 5 = 90%
    • Herbs have a 25% chance of success
    • Fungus has a 40% chance of success
    • Medicine plants have a 60% chance of success
    • Healing drafts have a 50% chance of success
    • Medicine has a 70% chance of success
    • Healing Potion has a 100% chance of success
