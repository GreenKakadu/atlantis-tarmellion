Seaman

Home to the seas, the seaman is the master of his ship and the waves. Sailing on the seas of Tarmellion, he is the master of this wet domain.
He can feed his crew from the bounty of the sea, sail and build the mighiest vessels and also knows how to defend his crew and ship remarkably well.
Seamen often have their own small retreats where they can outfit or build their ships and whereto they return after a profitable trade or looting venture.
