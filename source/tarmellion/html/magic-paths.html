Magic in Tarmellion

There are three paths of magic in Tarmellion. Almost all further magic study is based on one and only one of these paths.

The Path of Force

The Force Path is a path of Power. It deals with the astral force a mage may wield. A mage versed in this path can call down destructive magical forces on his enemies or shield his side against such attacks, he may control the weather or demons from the lower planes and do many other things.

Mages of the Force Path are often called Sorcerers.

The Path of Spirit

The Spirit Path is a spiritual path that deals with the mind and the soul. A mage versed in this path can discern hidden things, harm or trick the minds of other people and he may delve into the dark art of necromancy.

Mages of the Spirit Path are often called Priests or Enchanters.

The Path of Pattern

The Pattern Path is the magical way to influence matter. It changes one or more aspect of a given thing. A mage versed in this path can construct magical artifacts, use and build magical gates that span the world and bond with wild creatures.

Mages of the Pattern Path are often called Druids.
