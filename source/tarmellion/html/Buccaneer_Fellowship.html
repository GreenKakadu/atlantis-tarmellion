Buccaneer Fellowship

Geography:
The island of Tortuga is the seat of the famous brotherhood of pirates called the Buccaneer Fellowship and lies in the Mare Levanticus. The city of Bucc Den is a thriving harbour with drinking taverns, dockyards, warehouses and the homes of the wealthy captains. The rest of the island is either sheer rock or heavily wooded hills.

Government:
The leader of the Fellowship is called The Admiral and gest a share of all plunder or else!

Opportunities:
The wealth of seven seas and numerous coasts is there for the taking. All you need is a bit of courage, a good ship and a reasonably reliable crew.
