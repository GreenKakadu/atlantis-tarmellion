Magic Changes

Magic is one part of the game that was reworked thoroughly in Tarmellion as it was always the single most powerful choice in atlantis games. I have tried to balance it to the other choices war and trade while still keeping it interesting for magic interested players. The following things were done to the Magic:

  2. Limited Magic Users

    Only heroes (the starting characters of which each faction may have only one) are able to study all the magic paths and perfect them up to skill level 5.
    Each race has a racial leader that can study 6 magic skills (LHUMA and LHOBG only 4) which allow them to perfect about two magical uses. These leaders can study up to skill level 3.

  3. Spell Failure

    One of the changes done to the code is that every spell may now fail. This spell failure is listed with each spell description ingame. The caster will get a message that his spell has failed. The spell effect simply does not appear may this be the fire ball in a single round of battle or the magic sword after a month of labour.

  4. Multiple Mage Units

    To make studying for the vast amount of mages available in Tarmellion easier, they can be grouped together into unit of more than one mage. A magic-user is giveable in Tarmellion.

  5. New Magic Paths

    The structure of the magic skill dependancy has been changed. This is best seen on the page Magic Paths elsewhere on this homepage.

  6. New Magic Spells
    • ice - a combat spell similar to fire but inflicting weather damage
    • weather shield - a combat spell like energy shield to prevent magical weather damage
    • enchant rings - a spell to create magic rings that increase the skill level of its wearer
    • energy blast - a combat spell similar in destruction to call lightning but inflicting only energy damage
    • mind - a combat spell similar to fire but inflicting spirit damage
    • enchant amulets - a spell to create magic amulets that increase the skill level of its wearer
    • create charms - a spell to create magic charms that increase the skill level of its wearer
    • enchant armour - a spell to enfuse normal armour with added magic resistance
    • enchant weapons - a spell to enfuse normal weapons with magical armourpiercing abilities
    • create coronet of protection - a spell similar to create amulett of protection but giving weather protection
    • create staff of ice - a spell to create staves of ice based on the ice combat spell
    • create flaming sword - a spell to create a magical weapon with added fire damage
    • create ice sword - a spell to create a magical weapon with added weather damage
    • create soul eater - a spell to create a magical weapon with added spirit damage
    • wyrm lore - a spell foundation for the summon wyrm spell, used to limit the number of wyrms the caster of summon wyrm can have
    • summon dragon - a spell to summon good dragons to aid the caster
    • summon wyrm - a spell to summon evil wyrms to do the casters bidding

  7. Modified Magic Spells

    Many of the known spells were modified to balance them in the new structure. I will not list the exact modifications here as they are given in the spell descriptions ingame. Just a few notes about the biggest changes:
    dragon lore - is now a mere foundation skill for summon dragon
    summon balrog - can summon only one balrog which is not giveable
    create something - will probably have different output amounts than before as they were reworked and balanced
    wolf lore - is no longer dependant on the terrain and allows to summon either wolves or ice wolves depending on the skill level and the syntax of the3 cast command
