It might be helpful to inform yourself a bit about the game beforehand. There is a forum where players can talk to each other and where the ingame characters can interact. A special topic has been set aside to invite new players into existing alliances. Perhaps it is worth it to check them out. Just take a look here.

First you have to decide if you want to play a good or an evil character. Besides having effects on the roleplay of your character and faction this decides on which mayor continent or which part of the subterrean world your character will appear. It naturally also limits your choice of race to evil or good races.
