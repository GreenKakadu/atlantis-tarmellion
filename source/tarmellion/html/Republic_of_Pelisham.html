Republic of Pelisham

Geography:
Pel'Hash lies on the plains of Pelisham on the continent of Ashar next to the desert of Morella.

Government:
The Republic consists of twelve principalities that are ruled by the Guilds Council in Pel'Hash. Master Mages and Master Merchants both have a seat in this council and money and magic are its prime interests.

Opportunities:
You can be either rich and famous or poor and dead in Pel'Hash. Magic users, merchants and artificers from all lands flock to Pel'Hash to make their big deal. Some do even succeed.
