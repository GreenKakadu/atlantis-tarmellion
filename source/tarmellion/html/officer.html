Officer

A soldier from head to toe, the officer is the fundamental man-at-arms. Every nation of Tarmellion wants to have one of these gifted individuals in their pay to train their soldiers and then lead them to victory after victory.
His knowledge about all fighting styles and his strategic capabilities are exceptional.
Alas many officers use their power to carve their own realms out of the wilderness or even usurp the thrones of weaker rulers.
